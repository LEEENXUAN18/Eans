Criteria for Academic and Co-curricular Excellence Awards

Section Description Details
1.0 Academic Achievement Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) must be 3.60 or higher at the semester's end
2.0 Positions Held
2.1 Student Representative Council (SRC) Highest position held throughout the duration of study
2.2 Club/Association and Other Organizations Highest position held throughout the duration of study
3.0 Role in Program Organization
3.1 Program Organization Specify the role and level in program-related activities, such as research or competitions
4.0 Involvement/Participation
4.1 Activities Participation in academic activities, sports, cultural events, community service, entrepreneurship, and other student programs
5.0 Bonus
5.1 Professional Certification Recognitions from professional bodies (e.g., ACCA, CLP)
5.2 External Recognition Awards received from external organizations (e.g., Autodesk, CISCO)
5.3 International Involvement Participation in events or activities held abroad (e.g., International Design Fest)
6.0 Documentation Attach certified copies of relevant documents, including certificates and appointment letters, as proof of claims made in the application.
